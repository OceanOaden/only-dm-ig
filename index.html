IG DM Only

Check your Instagram DMs without the addictive feed

How it works

  1. Install — Add this app to your home screen
  2. Open — Tap the icon like any other app
  3. DMs — You're taken straight to your Instagram inbox

Install

iOS (Safari)

  1. Tap the Share button (square with arrow) at the bottom of Safari
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap "Add" in the top right

Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap the menu button (⋮) in the top right
  2. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  3. Tap "Add" to confirm

Desktop (Chrome / Edge)

  1. Click the install icon (⊕) in the address bar
  2. Click "Install" to confirm

Privacy

IG DM Only collects no data, requires no account, and has no tracking or analytics. It's a simple redirect to Instagram's DM page. The code is fully open source.
